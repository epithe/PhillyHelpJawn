PhillyHelpJawn

## Purpose
There are many people in Philadelphia who need help with basic needs such as access to food, shelter, employment seeking employment, etc. 
PhillyHelpJawn is an AI powered client on the cell phone device of the person needing help. PhillyHelpJawn uses a voice and graphic interface suitable for low literacy clients to assist them in discovering and navigating the services available

## Default user persona
The default user persona is a person who is functionally illiterate, has a low powered or basic mobile device, and has inconsistent access to wifi. 

A secondary persona is a person who works for a library or other agency whose role is to assist persons in need in some way.
